About the Akintunde Family

The Akintunde family carries a legacy of resilience, service and creativity. This site gathers biographies, old photographs, stories, and resources to preserve our lineage for future generations.

Curator's Note

Welcome — this site preserves the Akintunde heritage. If you have photos, documents, or stories to share, please use the Contact page to upload files and include any context or names. Submissions help build a richer family record.

Site Highlights

Family Tree

Interactive lineage view with generations, birthplaces and short bios. Start from the earliest known ancestors and navigate forward.

Photo Archive

Digitised photographs organised by decade and family branch. Each image includes captions and contributor credits where available.

Oral Histories

Transcribed interviews and audio snippets preserving memories, lessons and family stories for future listeners.

Documents & Records

Scanned certificates, letters and historic records with searchable metadata to help researchers and relatives.

Events Calendar

Upcoming reunions, memorials and community gatherings with RSVP and volunteer opportunities listed under Events.

Contribute & Upload

A simple contribution workflow lets family members upload photos, documents and stories on the Contact page for review and archiving.

How You Can Help

We welcome scanned photos, birth and marriage records, letters, voice recordings and short written recollections. When contributing, include names, dates and any identifying details. If a file is large, reach out via the Contact page so we can provide an alternate upload method.
